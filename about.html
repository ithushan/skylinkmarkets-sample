Our Story — Empowering Every Trader

Skylink Markets was founded on a simple principle: high-performance trading should be accessible, transparent, and built on institutional-grade technology.

A Legacy of Innovation

What started as a boutique firm in London's financial district has evolved into a global powerhouse in the brokerage industry. For over a decade, Skylink Markets has been at the forefront of digital transformation in financial services.

We recognized early on that the gap between professional institutions and individual retail traders was widening. Our journey began with the mission to bridge that gap, providing retail traders with the same execution speeds, liquidity depth, and analytical tools used by the world's leading hedge funds.
