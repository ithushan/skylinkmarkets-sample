Account Management

Information regarding verification, account types, and profile settings.

To comply with international financial regulations (KYC/AML), we require a valid government-issued photo ID (Passport, Driver’s License) and a proof of residence issued within the last 3 months (Utility bill, bank statement). Institutional accounts may require additional corporate documentation.

Most personal accounts are verified within 2 to 24 hours. Our compliance team reviews documents around the clock to ensure you can start trading as quickly as possible. You will receive an email notification as soon as your status is updated.
